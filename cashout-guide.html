Cashout Badge Guide

The tracker uses short badges so the cards stay clean. These badges are estimates based on available official pages, research reports, and player verification. They are not guarantees.

Pay speed badges

Instant-ish Usually reported or listed as near-instant after approval.

Fast Pay Usually same day or under 24 hours once verified.

Standard Pay Commonly 1–5 business days.

Slow Pay Often 3–10 business days or more.

Crypto Fast Crypto-based payout rails may process quickly, but wallet/network rules still matter.

Unknown Pay Not verified yet or reports conflict.

Method badges

Bank/Card Payouts appear to use bank, debit card, ACH, Skrill, PayPal, or similar rails.

Crypto Crypto payout is listed or strongly reported.

Hybrid More than one payout rail appears available.

Gift Card Gift card redemption is listed or commonly reported.

Unknown Method Method has not been verified yet.

Process badges

KYC Identity verification is required or strongly expected before cashout.

Same Method Rule Withdrawals may need to use the same payment rail used to buy coins.

First Cashout Slow First redemption may take longer due to added review.

Manual Review Payouts may be delayed by human review.

High Min Redeem Minimum redemption is high compared with other sites.

Crypto Only Crypto appears to be the only practical cashout rail.

Reality check

Casinos change payment processors, terms, redemption minimums, and KYC rules. A badge means “listed,” “reported,” or “player-verified at the time checked.” It does not mean a payout is guaranteed. Always check the casino’s current redemption page before depositing or relying on a cashout method.

Many sites prefer or require withdrawals through the same payment method used for purchases. That is usually tied to fraud prevention, chargebacks, payment processor rules, and anti-money-laundering controls.
